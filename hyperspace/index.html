Brain_Battle 2K22

IEEE SB TKMCE, ENCAMP 2.0 Phase 2 welcomes you for war, not of swords or guns, but to a “BRAIN BATTLE.” The event consists of four rounds- Logo identification, Scramble the word, Image Identification and Object finding. Based on overall performance, winners will be declared. Remember the judge’s decision will be final and crucial. The event will be held online and clues to be provided as PowerPoint. So what are you waiting for, unleash your rusty brain shackles and let the games begin!!!

About The Event

Round 1: Logo Identification

  • Two similar logos will be shown in the slide marked as one and two. Participants have to choose one option within 10 seconds. It will done using QUIZIZZ website.

Round 2:Scramble the Word

  • Tech related words will be given in jumbled format and clues related to that will be given one by one through ppt. Simultaneously students who cracked the word can enter their answers the website. Website link will be provided in the chat section.

Round 3:Image identification

  1. The participants will be given with clues in the form of pictures which are related to a famous tech personality or an organisation etc. and they will have to crack it.
  2. A partially covered picture of a person will be given ,from which the participants will have to guess the name of the personality.

Round 4: Object finding

  • Clues related to an object will be provided in the ppt and they have to click the picture of that object that submit it via website. Only easily accessible objects will be considered.
